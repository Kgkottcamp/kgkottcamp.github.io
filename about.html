Guy Named Kristian, Nice To Meet You!
I am a designer in the User Experience sphere with a knack for transforming complex ideas into intuitive, user-friendly, digital experiences.

My interest in UX design was sparked during my freshman year at Washington State University, where I was enrolled in the Digital Technology and Culture program. However, I found myself yearning for a more traditional design education, which led me to transfer to Western Washington University. Over the past years here, I have continued to immerse myself in the world of design in an effort to hone my skills.

I have worked on numerous projects, each presenting new challenges that have pushed me to think critically, approach problems from different perspectives, and design solutions that put the user first. Beyond the classroom, I have engaged in contract work to apply my skills in the real-world. These experiences have furthered my understanding of the design process and solidified my commitment to this exciting and ever-evolving field.

Within the last six months, I have been obsessed with the explosion of AI technology. Whether it be creating art with StableDiffusion, researching and running opensource Large Language Model models similar to ChatGPT on my PC, or learning how to animate using ControlNet functionality, I immerse myself in this emerging field as they will inevitably become a standard part of the design workflow.

When I am not researching and prototyping, you can find me exploring the beautiful Pacific Northwest, backpack equipped with the necessities, ready to unwind and enjoy Mother Nature. If it's raining like it usually does in the PNW, then forget about all that: give me a PlayStation controller or a mouse and keyboard and I'll be set.

As I prepare myself for the next steps of my professional journey in UX design, I am eager to work on projects that contribute positively to the world, where I can apply my skills and contribute in a team setting. I believe that a great user experience can truly transform how people interact with technology – and I am excited to be a part of that transformation.

If you have a project in mind, want to discuss design, or just want to recommend a good hike or game to play let me know! I am always up for a conversation!
